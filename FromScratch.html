Pros of Blu-Ray in general

  1. Superior audio Quality
    • Blu-Ray discs provide audio quality comparable to what is used at the movie studio. This results in an audio expereince that is much more detailed.
    • As a byproduct of this, surround sound effects are much more prevalant which makes the movies significantly more entertaining to listen to.
  2. Improved Visuals
    • Despite resolutions being the same (4k, 1080p etc), Blu-Ray discs provide a significantly higher video bitrate which results in improved details especially on larger screens. Bitrate is the amount of video data processed each second and is measured in megabits per second.
    • Most streaming services offer around a 15mbps bitrate, whereas standard 1080p HD and 4k UHD Blu rays offer up to 40 and 120mbps respectively. This often results in standard HD Blu ray movies having a better picture than their 4k streaming counterparts
  3. True Ownership
    • True Ownership is another huge benefit of Blu-Ray discs. On streaming services you dont own anything which means it can be taken away from you at anytime. With Blu-Ray, it is always there in the same place you left it in 5 years, a week, or just a few days.
    • This also adds the convenience factor of not having to hunt down and subscribe to A bunch of different services in order to watch all your favorite content. You will always have it.
  4. The Price
    • The average household spends roughly $75 monthly on streaming services. For that price you could buy 7 new HD blu rays every month or 4 new UHD blu rays. This gives you all afformentioned benefits and ends up being cheaper in the long term because once your collection is built up, you dont have to spend another penny.
    • The other benefit to this is there is no recurring charge to watch your content. You pay one time and it is yours forever. If while building your collection you decide not to buy any new movies that month you dont lose access to anything you have already bought.
